In lengthy YouTube videos, extracting specific information can be challenging. For example, a viewer might want to focus on topics such as

  1. Key concepts from a lecture
  2. Step-by-step tutorials in a coding video
  3. Insights from expert interviews
  4. Important moments in a documentary
  5. ... and many more.

Video Chat streamlines this process. We allow users to upload YouTube transcripts and engage in a conversation, providing answers while citing relevant video segments.

This helps students, professionals, and enthusiasts quickly find the information they need without watching the entire video.

Video Chat uses

  • Private LLMs. Data stays secure within the user's environment and is not used to train external models.
  • Function calling to retrieve and display specific video clips.
  • Ground-truthing by referencing exact timestamps and segments as evidence.
